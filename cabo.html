About Cabo

Cabo began as a fishing village it quickly became known as the Marlin capital of the world, now it is one of the main tourist destinations in Mexico and the world. Filled with adventure, fantasy and plenty of sun, there’s no wonder so many people visit Cabo each year.

With 360 days of sunshine, there is really no bad time to be in Cabo San Lucas. An array of water sports and outdoor activities, miles and miles of pristine beaches, championship golf courses designed by the industry’s best, and shopping, dining and entertainment catering to all tastes.

The old buildings of the historic center, the ever present stature of the beautiful church and the family friendly atmosphere that radiates around the town square, seem to make each of these descriptions accurate. San Jose del Cabo makes you feel a sense of tranquility into the desert landscape and the beautiful Sea of Cortez coastline. Old customs and traditions still remain and time seems to be nothing more than a symbol that applies to the rest of the world.

All visitors seem to feel that time goes by way too quickly during their visit.
